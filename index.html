About Us

At Enter Spain, our mission is simple: make your move to Spain easy, stress-free, and personalized. With over 1,500 clients served from more than 30 nationalities, our experience speaks for itself.

Founded by Ruben Oskam, our boutique team combines deep local knowledge with a proactive approach. Whether you’re relocating for work, retirement, or investment, we handle everything—from immigration and banking to housing and healthcare—so you can focus on settling in, not paperwork.

We connect you with trusted partners in real estate, insurance, banking, and accounting, crafting tailored solutions that align with your personal needs and ensure smooth transitions.
